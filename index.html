About us

At Maid for Me, we believe a clean space is the foundation for productivity, comfort, and peace of mind. Our Services

Regular Domestic Cleaning

Our regular domestic cleaning service keeps your home consistently fresh and tidy. Whether you need weekly or fortnightly visits, our professional cleaners handle dusting, vacuuming, kitchen and bathroom cleaning, and general upkeep to ensure a spotless living space.

Weekly Cleaning

A weekly cleaning service is perfect for maintaining a clean and hygienic home. It includes routine tasks such as dusting, mopping, wiping surfaces, and keeping high-traffic areas immaculate, preventing the buildup of dirt and grime.

Fortnightly Cleaning

Ideal for those who prefer a less frequent but thorough approach to home upkeep, our fortnightly cleaning covers all essential tasks, ensuring your home remains fresh and welcoming while allowing for deeper cleans when needed.

One-Off and Deep Cleanin

A one-off deep cleaning service is designed for homes that require a comprehensive and meticulous clean. We focus on neglected areas, including detailed kitchen and bathroom scrubbing, appliance cleaning, and tackling tough stains, ensuring a refreshed and revitalized living space.

End of Tenancy Cleaning

Our end-of-tenancy cleaning service ensures your property is spotless for the next occupants or a landlord inspection. It includes deep cleaning of every room, carpet and upholstery cleaning (if required), removing limescale, grease, and grime, leaving the space pristine and ready for new tenants.

Spring Cleaning

Our spring cleaning service offers a deep and refreshing cleanse for your home, perfect for seasonal resets. We focus on decluttering, dusting hard-to-reach places, washing windows, sanitizing surfaces, and revitalizing your living space, ensuring a fresh and organized environment.
